Introduction Trew

The Smith is a compendium class that introduces two new moves to Dungeon World and one new class.

New Tag

This is a new tag created for the Smith that is meant to show how incredibly sharp and pointy. The new tag, Razor-sharp has the following effect:

On a 10+ with Hack & Slash your attacks ignore armor.

New Moves

The two new moves are Sharpen Blade and its upgrade Blessed Blade.
